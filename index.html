Platform Capabilities

Everything an RN needs to manage
delegation — in one place.

Built specifically for Washington State nurse delegation workflows, DelegateRN replaces paper packets, fragmented files, and manual tracking with a unified clinical operating system.

⬡

Delegation Case Management

The core of the platform. Each case links an RN, caregiver, and patient with delegated tasks, instructions, compliance tracking, and full audit history.

⬡

DSHS Form Automation

All 11 official Washington State DSHS Nurse Delegation forms are derived from structured workflow data — never re-keyed, always accurate.

⬡

AFH Workspace System

Each Adult Family Home operates as its own isolated workspace containing caregivers, patients, delegation cases, documents, and visit history.

⬡

Caregiver Credential Tracking

Monitor training verifications, CPR certifications, competency assessments, and credential expiration across your entire caregiver panel.

⬡

Nursing Visit Workflow

Structured supervision visit documentation with competency reviews, observations, recommendations, and integrated reassessment scheduling.

⬡

Compliance Intelligence

Real-time compliance monitoring for expiring credentials, overdue reassessments, missing signatures, and incomplete delegation documentation.

⬡

Document Management

Centralized document storage with draft tracking, signed copy uploads, caregiver packet generation, and full version history per delegation case.

⬡

Patient Profile System

Comprehensive patient records with demographics, diagnoses, allergies, medications, emergency contacts, and complete delegation history.

⬡

Archive & Records

Permanently organized historical records for closed, rescinded, and completed delegation cases with full document and visit trail preservation.
