Generate 13-678 — Nurse Delegation: Consent for Delegation Process

Auto-fill from client profile — generates the DSHS consent form (Rev. 05/2025)

ℹ️ Select a client — fields 1–14 auto-populate. Obtain and record client or representative consent below before generating.
Client Identity — Fields 1–3
Client Contact — Fields 4–5
Medical Summary — Fields 6–7
Facility / Program Contact — Fields 8–11
12. Setting — Check One
Health Care Provider — Fields 13–14
Consent Statement (read to client before signing):
I have been informed the delegating Registered Nurse (RN) will only delegate to Long Term Care Workers (LTCW) who are capable and willing to properly perform the delegated nursing task(s). The LTCW must have an active Nursing Assistant or Home Care Aide - Certified credential. Nurse delegation will only occur after the delegating RN has assessed the ability of the Nursing Assistant or Home Care Aide to competently perform the delegated nursing task in the absence of direct or immediate nurse supervision and has completed required DSHS training per RCW 18.79.260 and WAC 246-840-930.
Fields 15–17 — Written / Signature Consent
⚠ Complete Fields 18–20 only if written consent was not obtained and verbal consent was given. Written consent must be obtained within 30 days of verbal consent.
Fields 18–20 — Verbal Consent (if applicable)
Fields 21–24 — Delegating RN
My signature below indicates that I have assessed this client and found his/her condition to be stable and predictable. I agree to provide nurse delegation per RCW 18.79 and WAC 246-840-910 through 970.

Exempt Long-Term Care Workers
